*CVPR 2026 (selected as highlight paper)*

## Overview

Pose-free 3D reconstruction from event camera streams without RGB input or external depth models.

## Key Contributions

- **Patch-based temporal coherence** for robust edge extraction from event streams
- **Edge-guided Gaussian initialization** for accurate scene representation
- **Edge-weighted reconstruction loss** for sharper geometry

## Datasets

- Replica (synthetic)
- TUM-VIE (real-world)
